Oye! My name is Liz Sarni and I'm a product & visual designer based in California.

Exploring the depths of both the print and digital realm, I create visually impactful designs that are inspired by eras of the past. Most days you can find me perfecting brand guidelines and web designs for a multitude of small companies that give love and attention to the way their brand looks and feels.

A little more info on my background is that I graduated from Loyola Marymount University in 2016 where I studied graphic design and film photography. I then made my way to earn a certificate from the Univeristy of California, Irvine in User Experience and Interface Design in 2018.
