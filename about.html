Liz is an agile and multidisciplinary designer who builds products for the web and tangible world. With a background in graphic design and user experience, she creates visually impactful designs and minimal interfaces that are inspired by eras of the past. Her solution-oriented design process and emphasis on color and typography is utilized throughout her range of work from brand identity to digital designs.

Some may or may not have surmised her work as the crème de la crème of design.
