Liz Sarni is a multidisciplinary designer inspired by eras of the past.

She graduated from Loyola Marymount University in 2016 where she studied graphic design and film photography. She went on to earn a certificate from the Univeristy of California, Irvine in User Experience and Interface Design in 2018. Exploring the depths of both the print and digital realm, she specializes in interface design, branding and editorial design.
