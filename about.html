Liz Sarni is a designer based in California.

Stay awhile.

Liz is currently an independent designer exploring both the print and digital realm. As a firm believer in utilizing simple design practices and crafting unique, minimal experiences, she continues to innovate and collaborate with teams on purposeful design.

Formerly the Visual Designer at Trafalgar and Graphic Designer at Gorjana, she's worked with various companies on finding and consistently representing their identity and presence in the world.

Much of her skillset relies heavily on research and iterations with emphasis on typography and colors. On most days, she specializes in user experience and visual identity.

In addition to her freelance, she's always looking for new opportunities or adventures to tackle.
