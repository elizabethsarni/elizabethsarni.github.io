ABOUT
LIZ SARNI

Product & Visual Designer

As a budding creative director, I tackle all design obstacles with research, collaboration, and constant iteration. Wether it’s user interface or graphics, I rely heavily on design principles and best practices with the end user in mind. As you look through my work, you will find clean designs inspired by eras of the past. Stay awhile.
