Liz Sarni is multidisciplinary designed based in California.

Exploring the depths of both the print and digital realm, Liz creates visually impactful designs that are inspired by eras of the past. She graduated Loyola Marymount University after having studied graphic design and film photography. She then made her way to earn a certificate from the Univeristy of California, Irvine in User Experience and Interface Design.
