Liz Sarni is multidisciplinary designer living and working in California.

About

Exploring the depths of both the print and digital realm, Liz creates visually impactful designs that are inspired by eras of the past.

She graduated from Loyola Marymount University in Los Angeles, where she studied graphic design and film photography. She went on to earn a certificate in User Experience and Interface Design from the Univeristy of California, Irvine. Although much of her experience is in editorial and web design, she has a particular passion for brand identity and user interface design.
