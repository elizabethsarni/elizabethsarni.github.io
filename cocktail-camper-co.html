Cocktail Camper Co.

branding | art direction | web design

Cocktail Camper Co. is a mid-century modern bar concept brought to life in Austin, Texas. Through events, fundraisers, and at home parties, their bartenders and delicious ingredients are brought directly to you and served best from their vintage airstream camper.

Their brand identity utilizes playful colors and rounded corners. Typography and various iconography are incorporated throughout their print and digital assets. Website development is currently underway.

YEAR: 2018 - 2019
