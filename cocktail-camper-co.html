Cocktail Camper Co.

Libation Lovers Only

Cocktail Camper Co. is a mid-century modern bar concept currently being brought to life in Austin, Texas. Their sole reason for existence: libations. Through events, fundraisers, and at home parties, their bartenders and delicious ingredients are brought directly to you and are served best from their vintage airstream camper. Combining clean iconography with playful colors and a mid-century modern aesthetic, the branding is a reflection of the personalities behind the bar and the energy they bring to any party. Although a custom website concept was presented, it was not carried out due to budget.
