Gorjana x Emails

email design | art direction

Gorjana is a jewelry company based in Laguna Beach, California. Much of their jewelry and brand identity surrounds women channeling their strength and empowerment through gemstones.

As the sole designer on the team, I created a series of emails that each highlighted a specific theme and popular category of jewelry. My email strategy, content creation, and collaboration with the photographer increased gorjana's click-through rate by 12%.
