Talent Connection Group

branding | web design

Talent Connection Group is a boutique recruitment firm based in Austin, Texas. Although they specialize in payments companies, they make a presence in providing any team with top talent.

Through cleaning up the color palette and simplifying the logo, a more trusting and modern branding was created. The blue hues and sans-serif typeface was carried out consistently from business cards to web design. The website is currently under development.

YEAR: 2018 - 2019
