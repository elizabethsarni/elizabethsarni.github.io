Talent Connection Group

Connecting Top Talent with Great Companies.

Talent Connection Group is a boutique recruitment firm based in Austin, Texas. Although they specialize in payments companies, they make a presence in providing any team with top talent. Through cleaning up the color palette and simplifying the logo, a more trusting and modern branding was created. The blue hues and sans-serif typeface were carried out consistently from business cards to web design to keep a clean and elevated look. The website is currently under development.
