This Mama Needs Grace

Flourish, Baby

This Mama Needs Grace is an open diary blog that connects motherhood and faith and the life lessons that come with it. Primary and secondary logo stamps were created with the same goal and feeling in mind: flourish. The logo plays with both vintage botanical illustrations and traditional-inspired typefaces.

Secondary collateral such as business cards and website concept were created but not used in the end.
